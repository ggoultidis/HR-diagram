Stellar Evolution tracks of binary systems

by George Goultidis

In order to see the stellar evolution track, you firstly need to select the binary system. The first number is the mass of the donor star. The second is the mass of the accretor star. Both are in solar masses. The third number is directly correlated to their period.

Secondly, you will need to select whether to follow the donor star or the accretor.

Finally, you can play around with the Start, Stop and Clear buttons
